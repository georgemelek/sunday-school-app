Privacy Policy

MinistryHub  ·  Effective date: July 14, 2026

MinistryHub ("the app", "we", "us") is a tool for Coptic Orthodox youth ministry servants and coordinators to manage class schedules, student rosters, attendance, and outreach. We take the privacy of everyone in the ministry — especially children — seriously.

1. Who We Are

MinistryHub is developed and operated by George Melek, independently, for use by Coptic Orthodox churches. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by any official Coptic body. For privacy questions, contact: melekgeorge@outlook.com.

2. Who Uses the App

The app is used exclusively by adult volunteers ("servants") and ministry leaders ("coordinators"). Children do not have accounts and do not directly interact with the app. Student information is entered by servants on behalf of their ministry.

3. Information We Collect

3a. Servant / Coordinator Account Data

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Role (servant or coordinator)
  • Church affiliation

3b. Student Data (entered by servants)

  • Student name and gender
  • Date of birth
  • Student phone number (optional)
  • Parent/guardian names, email addresses, and phone numbers
  • Home address (street, city, state, zip)
  • Notes entered by servants

3c. Activity Data

  • Attendance records (which students were present at which sessions)
  • Servant availability (dates marked available or unavailable)
  • Outreach visit logs (dates and notes from home/remote visits)
  • Session and curriculum data (lesson topics, locations, schedules)

4. How We Use This Information

  • To provide the core features of the app (schedules, attendance, outreach tracking)
  • To allow coordinators to oversee ministry operations within their church
  • To authenticate users via magic link email

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use student data for any purpose other than facilitating ministry operations.

5. Who Can See Your Data

  • Servants can see student data for grades they serve, their own availability, and session info for classes they're assigned to.
  • Coordinators can see all grades, students, servants, and sessions within their church.
  • Data is strictly scoped per church. No servant or coordinator can see data from another church.
  • MinistryHub staff (currently: the developer) can access data for technical support and maintenance. This access is limited and logged.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure in the United States.
  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Row-level security policies in the database enforce the access rules described in Section 5.
  • Authentication uses passwordless magic links — we never store passwords.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

MinistryHub does not permit children under 13 to create accounts. Children are represented in the app only as student roster entries, entered by adult servants. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us immediately at melekgeorge@outlook.com and we will delete it.

Parents or guardians who wish to review, correct, or delete their child's information stored in the app should contact the ministry coordinator at their church, or email us directly.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Stream — in-app messaging. Stream Privacy Policy.
  • Expo / EAS — app build and distribution infrastructure.

We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms.

9. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Student data entered by servants is retained until the servant or coordinator deletes it, or the church's data is removed from the system. If you delete your account, your profile is permanently deleted. Student records you entered remain unless a coordinator removes them.

10. Your Rights

  • You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • You may request correction or deletion of your account data.
  • You may delete your own account from Settings → Delete Account at any time.

To exercise these rights, email melekgeorge@outlook.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notice and a new effective date on this page.
